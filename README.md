# Electric Vehicle Market Analysis & Forecast – India

## Project Overview:
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of the Indian Electric Vehicle (EV) market to support strategic decision-making for **AtliQ Motors**, a global EV manufacturer planning to expand into India.

The dashboard analyzes historical data (FY 2022–2024) and provides future projections up to **2030**. The objective is to understand market size, growth trends, regional opportunities, competitive landscape, and future demand.

## Business Problem:
AtliQ Motors currently holds less than 2% market share in India and lacks clarity on:
- Overall EV market growth and potential
- State-wise adoption and high-opportunity regions
- Competitive positioning of existing manufacturers
- Demand trends and seasonality
- Future market outlook
This project delivers data-driven insights to support market entry and expansion strategy.

## Objectives:
- Analyze overall EV market size and penetration
- Identify high-performing and high-potential states
- Evaluate manufacturer performance and market concentration
- Understand demand patterns and seasonal trends
- Forecast EV sales growth at the state level up to 2030
- Provide strategic insights for business decision-making

## Tools & Technologies:
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development and visualization
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- **DAX** – Measures and calculated metrics
- **Excel / CSV** – Data storage

## Key Insights:
- EV sales exceeded **2 million units** between 2022–2024.
- National EV penetration is still low (~3–4%), indicating strong growth potential.
- **2-Wheelers dominate** the EV market in terms of volume.
- The market is highly concentrated, with **Top 5 manufacturers holding ~63% share**.
- EV adoption is strongest in states like **Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Delhi**.
- The market is projected to grow significantly to **~50 million units by 2030**.
- Demand shows clear seasonal patterns, useful for production and inventory planning.

## Business Impact:
This dashboard helps AtliQ Motors:
- Identify high-priority states for market entry
- Understand competitive intensity
- Choose the right vehicle segment strategy
- Estimate future demand and growth opportunities
- Make data-driven expansion decisions
